History

I grew up in the village of Mahasenpura in Welikanda, a village beyond Polonnaruwa. As a child I was educated at Mahasenpura Primary School. Then I went to Aselapura Maha Vidyalaya to do O / L. After that I entered Welikanda Central College for A / L education. During this educational journey, my education focused on various industrial innovations. I wanted to go to a university for further education. My marks were not enough to go to government universities. I decide to sign up for esoft metro campus. There I was studying for an honors degree in Information Technology.
